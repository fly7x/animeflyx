Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers — more in our Discord.

Is AnimeFly free?+
Yes — 100% free. No subscription, no premium tier, no credit card. Ever.
Do I need an account?+
No account needed. Stream anything instantly. Your Continue Watching list is saved locally in your browser — zero data sent anywhere.
Are there any ads?+
AnimeFly runs zero platform ads. Some embedded stream servers may occasionally show a brief prompt — switching to another server removes this instantly.
What if a server won't load?+
Try Server 2 or Server 3 in the player controls. Also toggle Sub ↔ Dub. Report persistent issues in our Discord.
How fast are new episodes added?+
Ongoing series are typically available within 1–3 hours of Japanese broadcast. Join Discord for real-time alerts.
Is Sub and Dub available?+
Yes. Use the SUB/DUB toggle. If Dub isn't available, the stream automatically falls back to Sub.
Is it mobile friendly?+
Absolutely. AnimeFly is fully responsive and tested on every screen from 320px phones to 4K monitors.
