Is AnimeFly free?
Yes — 100% free. No subscription, no premium tier, no card required. Ever.
Do I need an account?
No account needed. Your Continue Watching history saves locally in your browser — no login, nothing sent anywhere.
What if a server won't load?
Click Server 2 or Server 3 in the player. Also toggle Sub ↔ Dub to force a reload. Report persistent issues on Discord.
Are there ads?
AnimeFly has zero ads. Some embedded stream servers may occasionally show a prompt — switching servers removes this.
