Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers — more in our Discord.

Is AnimeFlyX free?+
Yes — 100% free. No subscription, no premium tier, no credit card. Ever.
Do I need an account?+
No account needed. Stream anything instantly. Your continue watching list is saved locally in your browser.
Are there ads?+
AnimeFlyX runs zero platform ads. Some embedded stream servers may occasionally show a brief prompt — toggling SUB/DUB usually removes this.
What if the player won't load?+
Toggle SUB ↔ DUB to force a reload. If it still doesn't work, the episode may not be available yet. Report issues in our Discord.
How fast are new episodes added?+
Ongoing series are typically available within 1–3 hours of broadcast. Join Discord for real-time alerts.
Is Sub and Dub available?+
Yes. Use the SUB/DUB toggle on the player. If Dub isn't available, the stream falls back to Sub automatically.
Is it mobile friendly?+
Absolutely. AnimeFlyX is fully responsive and tested on phones from 320px width up to 4K monitors.
