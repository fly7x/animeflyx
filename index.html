Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers — more in our Discord.

Is AnimeFly free?+
Yes — 100% free. No subscription, no premium tier, no credit card. Ever.
Do I need an account?+
No account needed. Stream anything instantly. Your Continue Watching list is saved locally in your browser — zero data is sent anywhere.
Are there any ads?+
AnimeFly runs zero platform ads. Some embedded servers may occasionally show a brief prompt — switching to another server removes this instantly.
What if a server won't load?+
Try Server 2 or Server 3 in the player controls. Also try toggling Sub ↔ Dub. If all 3 fail, disable browser extensions or switch to Chrome/Firefox. Report persistent issues in our Discord.
How quickly are new episodes added?+
Ongoing series are typically available within 1–3 hours of the original Japanese broadcast. Join our Discord for real-time new episode alerts.
Are Sub and Dub both available?+
Yes. Use the SUB/DUB toggle in the player. If Dub isn't available for a title, the stream automatically falls back to Sub.
Is it mobile friendly?+
Absolutely. AnimeFly is fully responsive and tested on every screen size from 320px phones to 4K monitors.
